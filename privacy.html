Privacy Policy

Your privacy is the whole point

Last updated: April 26, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Short version: webp.convert runs entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device. We don't have servers processing your images, and we don't collect personal data.

01

What we collect

We collect nothing. webp.convert does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking of individual users.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact details
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Device identifiers or browser fingerprints
  • Usage patterns or behavioral data
  • Payment information of any kind
02

Your files & images

Your files never leave your device. Image conversion happens entirely inside your web browser using the built-in Canvas API. No file is uploaded to any server at any point.

When you drop a ZIP file onto webp.convert:

  • The file is read locally by your browser using the JavaScript File API
  • Images are decoded and re-encoded entirely within your browser tab
  • The output ZIP is generated in memory and downloaded directly to your device
  • Nothing is transmitted over the network

Once you close or refresh the tab, all file data is permanently gone from the browser's memory. We have no copy, no record, and no access to anything you processed.

03

Cookies & local storage

webp.convert does not use cookies. We do not use local storage, session storage, or IndexedDB to store any information about you or your usage.

Your browser settings (such as quality preference or max width) are not saved between sessions — this is intentional, as it means nothing about your session persists.

04

Third-party services

webp.convert loads two external resources:

  • Google Fonts — loads the Lato typeface from fonts.googleapis.com. Google may log this font request per their own Privacy Policy. No personal data specific to you is shared.
  • JSZip — loaded from the Cloudflare CDN (cdnjs.cloudflare.com). This is a JavaScript library that runs entirely in your browser. Cloudflare may log the CDN request per their own Privacy Policy.

These are standard CDN requests comparable to loading any stylesheet or script on any website. Neither service receives your images or file data.

05

Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Hotjar, or any other analytics or tracking tool. There is no pixel tracking, session recording, or heatmap software on this site.

If hosted on a platform such as GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel, that platform may collect basic server-level access logs (page views, IP addresses) as part of their standard infrastructure. This is governed by that platform's privacy policy, not ours.

06

Children's privacy

webp.convert is a general-purpose developer tool and is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no personal information whatsoever, there is no special risk to children using this service. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, regardless of age.

07

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly — we recommend bookmarking this page if you want to track changes.

Continued use of webp.convert after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
